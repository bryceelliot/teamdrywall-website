FAQ

Common Questions

Do you serve areas outside Kelowna?

Yes — Team Drywall Ltd. operates throughout BC and Alberta. We regularly work in the Okanagan region and take on projects province-wide for larger commercial clients.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out our online quote request form or call us directly at 250-807-2700. We respond promptly and can typically provide an estimate within 1–2 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cheque and email money transfer (Interac e-Transfer).

Do you do emergency drywall repairs?

Yes. We understand that water damage and other emergencies can't wait. Call us at 250-807-2700 and we'll do our best to respond quickly.

Are you licensed and insured in BC?

Yes. Team Drywall Ltd. is fully licensed and insured to operate in British Columbia and Alberta. We carry appropriate liability coverage on all projects, giving you full peace of mind.

How long does a typical drywall project take?

It depends on the scope. A small repair can be done in a single day. A full basement or room renovation typically takes 3–5 days. Large commercial projects are scheduled around your construction timeline. We always give you a clear schedule before we start — and we stick to it.

What finish level do you recommend for my home?

For most residential applications, Level 4 is standard and suits flat or matte paints. For high-sheen paints, accent walls, or premium interiors, we recommend Level 5 — the smoothest possible finish. We'll advise you during your free quote based on your paint and lighting plan.

Can you match existing texture on a repair?

Absolutely. Texture matching is one of our specialties. Whether your walls have orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, or a smooth finish, our finishers blend repairs so cleanly you won't be able to tell where the patch is.
